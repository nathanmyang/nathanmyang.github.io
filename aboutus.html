About Us

Overview

MacroStat provides statistical support, data management, and report writing services to the pharmaceutical, bio-technology, and medical device industries, as well as the National Institutes of Health (NIH).

MacroStat has provided statistical support for many Data and Safety Monitoring Board (DSMB) and FDA Advisory Committee meetings.

We have extensive experience in various therapeutic areas such as pain control, oncology, cardiovascular, respiratory, psychotic disorders, anti-viral, antibiotic, and GI.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide high quality, efficient, and cost-effective services.

Our Clients

We have provided quality and successful services to the large pharmaceutical, bio-technology, and medical device companies, and the NIH since 2002.
