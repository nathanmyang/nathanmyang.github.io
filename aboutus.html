About Our Business

MacroStat provides statistical analysis, data management, and report writing services to the pharmaceutical, bio-tech, and medical device industries, and National Institutes of Health (NIH).

MacroStat is specialized in statistical support for the Data and Safety Monitoring Board (DSMB) and FDA Advisory Committee meetings.

We have extensive experience in various therapeutic areas such as pain control, oncology, cardiovascular, respiratory, psychotic disorders, anti-viral, antibiotic, and GI.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide fast, high quality, and cost-effective services.

Our Clients

We provide quality and successful services to the large pharmaceutical, bio-tech, and medical device companies, and the NIH.
