Bing Zhang, Ph.D.
President

Dr. Zhang has a Ph.D. in Statistics and a M.S. in Computer Science from the University of Kentucky. He has fifteen years of experience in line management and statistical analyses for pharmacokinetic (PK) and Phase I-IV clinical trials as well as integrated summaries of safety (ISS) and efficacy (ISE) for new drug applications (NDA) in various therapeutic areas such as pain control, oncology, cardiovascular, respiratory, psychotic disorders, anti-viral, antibiotic, and blood substitute. Before founding MacroStat Inc., he held the position of Associate Director of Biostatistics at a biotech company, various leadership positions for statistics and SAS programming groups at a large pharmaceutical company, and Senior Statistician at a CRO. Dr. Zhang has extensive experience in the development of clinical development plans, clinical trial protocols, statistical analysis plans, and clinical trial reports; and in the preparation of multiple NDA submissions. He has received multiple commendations for his statistical work. He also designed and developed a PC-based computer-aided NDA (CANDA) that was submitted to Cardio-Renal division and resulted in acceleration of NDA approval.

Helen Yin, M.S.
Managing Director of MacroStat China

Ms. Yin has a M.S. in Biostatistics from the University of Massachusetts. She has more than twelve years of experience in management and statistical analyses in the pharmaceutical industry worldwide, including eight years in United States and four years in China/Asia Region. She has held the positions of Head of Clinical Support Division of the R&D Department, Associate Director of Biostatistics, US therapeutic leader for breast cancer, and other statistical and consulting positions in the pharmaceutical and CRO companies. She has extensive experience in multiple IND/NDA submissions in various therapeutic areas, especially oncology.
