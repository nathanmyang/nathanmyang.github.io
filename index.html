Overview

MacroStat, Inc. is an international contract research organization (CRO), providing statistical analysis, data management, and report writing services to the pharmaceutical, bio-tech, and medical device industries, and National Institutes of Health (NIH). MacroStat has offices in Delaware, US and Shanghai, China.

We provide high quality services since 2002.

Specialized Areas:

  • SAP development and SAS programming
  • DSMB and interim analyses
  • Generation of CDISC SDTM and ADaM datasets
